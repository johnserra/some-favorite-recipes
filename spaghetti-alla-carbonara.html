Spaghetti Alla Carbonara

John Serra

One of the four Roman pasta dishes that is quick, simple, and yet delicious to make. I like the original recipe prepared with guanciale (or pancetta), but I live in Istanbul, Turkey where they are hard to come by. So, I have modified the recipe to use Turkish Kavurma which is braised beef and lamb with lamb tail fat packed in a casing (non edible) like sausage.
